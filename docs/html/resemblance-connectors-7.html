Solar System (Extract)

The Solar System is the Sun and all the objects in orbit around it. The Sun is orbited by planets, asteroids, comets and other things. Six of the planets and three of the dwarf planets are orbited by moons. Furthermore, planetary dust orbits the gas giants.
