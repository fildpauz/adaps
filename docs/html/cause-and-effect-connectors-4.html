Mussel (Extract)

Mussels are several families of bivalve molluscs. Mussels live in lakes, rivers, and creeks. They sometimes also live in intertidal areas along coastlines worldwide. The freshwater mussels and saltwater mussels are not closely related, despite the fact that they may look similar.
